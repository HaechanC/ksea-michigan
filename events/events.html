Social

Here are some pictures from past social events 🧺

Game Night

Game night is a night full of fun games, prizes to win, and people to meet! You're able to play the games in teams and bond with your teammates.

Picnic Social

When the weather is warm in the beginning of the year, we always host a picnic social with yummy snacks and games.

Caffeine Chats

This is an event where we provide caffeinated drinks and let you get to know board members. It is especially helpful if you want to talk to board members to meet them, catch up with them, or even ask them for advice on anything!

Professional

Here are some pictures from past professional events 👩🏻‍💻

Backpacking Events

We host this event every semester during backpacking season. Our board has a variety of STEM majors you can ask for advice on which classes to choose. We also offer career advice and four-year plan advice in a casual setting.

Build Your Profile Workshop

This workshop is meant to teach attendees how to build a professional profile, where topics we help with range from recruiting for internships, resume or LinkedIn building, and general professional advice.

Headshot Events

We help you get a professional headshot for free! We usually host this event in the fall semester.
